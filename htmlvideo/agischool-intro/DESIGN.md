## Style Prompt

AGISchool 品牌视频。紫色基调，温暖而专业，面向教育领域。以深紫色为主色调，传达科技与教育融合的理念。整体氛围是自信、温暖、启发性的——不是冰冷的科技风，而是"AI赋能教育"的人文感。

## Colors

- `#1A1230` — Background (深紫黑，比纯黑更有品牌感)
- `#FFFFFF` — Primary text (白色，在深色背景上的高对比)
- `#7260AF` — Accent (品牌紫，来自网站 nav active 色)
- `#8C81D3` — Secondary accent (亮紫，来自网站 footer 链接色)
- `#49406F` — Heading tint (深紫灰，来自网站标题色)
- `rgba(140, 129, 211, 0.3)` — Decorative glow (品牌紫低透明度)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** "DM Sans", sans-serif — weight 800
- **Body/Labels:** "IBM Plex Sans", sans-serif — weight 400

## Motion

- Calm-to-medium energy: 0.4-0.7s entrances
- `power2.out` and `sine.inOut` as primary eases
- Blur crossfade transitions between scenes (0.5s)
- Ambient elements: slow breathing glows, drifting decorative text

## What NOT to Do

- No neon or electric colors — this is education, not gaming
- No aggressive/fast transitions — keep it warm and inviting
- No gradient text effects
- No pure #000 backgrounds — always tinted purple
- No more than 2 font families

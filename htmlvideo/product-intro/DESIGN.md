## Style Prompt

Clean, precise, data-driven. Light canvas with structured layout, grid-locked feel. Inspired by Swiss Pulse but adapted for a light background — clinical clarity with controlled accent color. Typography is sharp and deliberate. Motion is snappy, confident, and functional.

## Colors

- `#F5F5F0` — Background (warm off-white, avoids sterile pure white)
- `#1B1B1B` — Primary text (near-black, tinted warm)
- `#0052CC` — Accent (confident blue, not generic — leans cobalt)
- `#6B7280` — Secondary text (cool mid-gray)
- `#E2E5EA` — Structural elements (rules, dividers, subtle panels)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** "DM Sans", sans-serif — weight 800
- **Body/Labels:** "IBM Plex Mono", monospace — weight 400

## Motion

- Snappy entrances: 0.3-0.5s, `expo.out` and `power3.out`
- Offset first animation 0.2s from scene start
- Vary entrance directions: y, x, scale, opacity
- Medium-energy transitions: blur crossfade at 0.4s

## What NOT to Do

- No gradient text or decorative gradients
- No cyan-on-dark or neon accents
- No centered-and-floating layouts — anchor to edges, use split frames
- No identical animation patterns within a scene
- No pure `#000` or `#fff` — always tinted
